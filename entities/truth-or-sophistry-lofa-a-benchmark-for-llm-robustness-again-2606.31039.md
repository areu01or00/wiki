# Truth or Sophistry? LoFa: A Benchmark for LLM Robustness Against Logical Fallacies

## Overview
LoFa is a comprehensive benchmark evaluating LLM robustness against fallacious persuasion — whether LLMs can resist being swayed by manipulative linguistic patterns that exploit logical weaknesses. Uses a multi-agent pipeline pairing factual questions with fallacious arguments, plus a multi-round debate framework. Separates fallacy resistance from inherent knowledge by proposing Logical Fallacy Resistance at k (LFR@k).
