# Scalable Variational Bayesian Fine-Tuning of LLMs via Orthogonalized Low-Rank Adapters

## Overview
When deploying LLMs to safety-critical applications, uncertainty quantification (UQ) is paramount for self-assessment. However, decisions suffer from overconfidence after PEFT on domain-specific tasks. This paper introduces scalable variational Bayesian fine-tuning via orthogonalized low-rank adapters — enabling principled uncertainty estimation without sacrificing efficiency.
