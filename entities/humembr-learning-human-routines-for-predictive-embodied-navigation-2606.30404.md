# HUMEMBR: Learning Human Routines for Predictive Embodied Navigation

## Overview
HUMEMBR tackles a fundamental challenge in robotics: understanding and navigating human-centered environments over extended periods while modeling human behavior and routines. In real-world settings, robots must locate specific individuals and predict where they are likely to be — requiring both physical navigation and behavioral modeling of human patterns.

## Key Contributions
- Human routine modeling for navigation: learns temporal patterns of human movement to predict future locations
- Predictive embodied navigation: robot anticipates where humans will be, not just where they are
- Long-horizon environmental understanding in human-centered spaces
- Joint behavioral + physical navigation planning
