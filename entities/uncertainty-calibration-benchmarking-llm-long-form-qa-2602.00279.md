# Title:Benchmarking Uncertainty Calibration in Large Language Model Long-Form Question Answering

## Overview
Large Language Models are increasingly deployed in Question Answering settings, including natural science domains. Reliable Uncertainty Quantification (UQ) is critical for trustworthy uptake of generated answers. This paper introduces the first large-scale benchmark for evaluating UQ metrics in reasoning-demanding QA, studying calibration across multiple LLMs.

## Key Contributions
- First large-scale benchmark for evaluating uncertainty quantification in long-form scientific QA
- Systematic study of calibration across multiple LLM architectures
- Analysis of reasoning-demanding QA vs fact-retrieval QA calibration differences
