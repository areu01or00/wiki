# A Unified Pair-GRPO Family: From Implicit to Explicit Preference Constraints for Stable and General RL Alignment

## Overview
This paper addresses critical instability issues in LLM alignment via RLHF, where mainstream pairwise preference learning paradigms suffer from unstable policy updates, ambiguous gradient directions, poor interpretability, and high gradient variance. The authors propose a unified Pair-GRPO family that systematically transitions from implicit to explicit preference constraints, enabling more stable and generalizable RL alignment across diverse task distributions.
