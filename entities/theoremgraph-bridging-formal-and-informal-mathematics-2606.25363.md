# TheoremGraph: Bridging Formal and Informal Mathematics

## Overview
Introduces a unified statement-level dependency graph spanning informal (11.7M math arXiv theorems) and formal (Coq, Isabelle, Lean) mathematics, enabling cross-repository theorem discovery and retrieval.
