# Safe Online Learning via Smooth Safety-Structured Policy Composition

## Overview
AutoSafe integrates structured safety monitoring directly into the action generation process — enabling smooth risk-dependent transitions between performance-driven and safety-preserving behaviors without the discontinuities of traditional intervention-based safety enforcement.
