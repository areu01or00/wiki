# Harnessing Textual Refusal Directions for Multimodal Safety

## Overview
This paper extends refusal direction techniques from text-only LLM alignment to the multimodal setting — demonstrating that textual refusal directions can be projected into multimodal activation space to improve safety in vision-language models. Shows feasibility of transferring alignment mechanisms across modalities without full retraining.

## Key Facts
- **Authors**: D'Incà, Moreno; Mancini, Massimiliano; Sebe, Nicu
- **Year**: 2026
- **arXiv**: [2606.31876](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31876)

## Key Contributions
- First work transferring textual refusal directions to multimodal (VLM) safety
- Demonstrates cross-modal projection of alignment mechanisms without retraining
- Novel technique for multimodal safety that avoids expensive full-model alignment
- **First refusal-direction transfer from text to multimodal in the wiki.**
