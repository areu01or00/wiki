# From Reasoning Traces to Reusable Modules: Understanding Compositional Generalization in Language Model Reasoning

## Overview
Post-training pipelines combining supervised fine-tuning (SFT) with reinforcement learning (RL) have emerged as the key recipe for transforming LLMs into robust reasoners. This paper argues that this combined success is driven by compositional generalization, formalized through a hierarchical latent selection model where reasoning traces are generated compositionally from reusable modules. The work provides both an interpretable framework and an empirical account of why SFT+RL works for reasoning.
