# Better Think Thrice: Learning to Reason Causally with Double Counterfactual Consistency

## Overview
This paper identifies a key weakness in LLMs: brittleness under counterfactual questions, suggesting causal reasoning is shallow. It introduces Double Counterfactual Consistency — training LLMs to handle counterfactual queries by ensuring they reason correctly about both what happened and what could have happened.

- **arXiv**: [2602.16787](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16787)

## Key Contributions
- Double Counterfactual Consistency training for causal reasoning robustness
- Labeled counterfactual task generation at scale
- Evaluates causal reasoning ability beyond standard benchmarks
