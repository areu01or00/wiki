# Diffusion Fine-tuning with Rewarded Moment Matching Distillation

## Overview
This paper investigates the interaction between distillation and reinforcement learning fine-tuning in diffusion models — two pillars of diffusion post-training that are traditionally studied in isolation. The authors propose a rewarded moment matching distillation approach that bridges these phases, showing improved alignment and generation quality compared to sequential or isolated application of these techniques.

## Key Contributions
- First systematic study of distillation-RL interaction in diffusion post-training
- Rewarded moment matching distillation framework bridging the two phases
- Empirical results showing improved alignment and generation quality
- Analysis of how RL signals interact with learned latent representations during distillation
