# Cross-Lingual Exploration for Parametric Knowledge

## Overview
Parametric knowledge in Large Language Models is not equally accessible across languages. This work investigates techniques for accessing hidden factual knowledge via cross-lingual prompting strategies, identifying four inherent dimensions of cross-lingual exploration that govern parametric knowledge retrieval. Evaluated on multilingual factual benchmarks across 17 typologically diverse languages, demonstrating that cross-lingual exploration significantly improves knowledge transfer and factual recall as a compute Pareto frontier vs. native-language scaling.

## Key Facts
- **Authors**: Cross-Lingual Exploration team

## Key Contributions
- Four inherent dimensions of cross-lingual exploration that govern parametric knowledge retrieval in LLMs
- Multilingual factual benchmarks covering 17 typologically diverse languages
- Cross-lingual exploration as compute Pareto frontier vs. native-language scaling (more efficient than scaling model size)
- Improved cross-lingual consistency exceeding what accuracy gains alone would predict
- Inference-time strategy: unlocks latent parametric knowledge without fine-tuning
