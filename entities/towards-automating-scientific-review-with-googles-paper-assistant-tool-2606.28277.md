# Towards Automating Scientific Review with Google's Paper Assistant Tool

## Overview
As AI-accelerated science produces papers faster than human reviewers can evaluate them, this paper investigates deploying AI itself as a scientific peer reviewer. Google's Paper Assistant Tool is used to automate review generation, citation verification, and methodological critique — addressing the systemic bottleneck of traditional human peer review at AI-accelerated publication volumes.

## Key Facts
- **Authors**: Jayaram, Rajesh; Tyler, Drew; Woodruff, David + 4
- **Year**: 2026
- **arXiv**: [2606.28277](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28277)

## Key Contributions
- Automated scientific peer review using LLM agents (Google Paper Assistant Tool)
- Citation consistency verification and methodological critique generation
- Analysis of systemic bottlenecks: human peer review cannot scale to AI-accelerated publication volume
- Deploy AI to accelerate review alongside AI-accelerated science
