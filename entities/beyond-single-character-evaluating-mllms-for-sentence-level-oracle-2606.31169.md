# Beyond Single Character: Evaluating MLLMs for Sentence-Level Oracle Bone Inscription Understanding

## Overview
S-OBI (Sentence-level Oracle Bone Inscription benchmark) evaluates multimodal LLMs at the sentence level rather than individual character recognition. Prior OBI work focused on character-level recognition; S-OBI captures the long-form textual coherence and contextual dependencies embedded in complete divination charges — a qualitatively different capability that tests whether MLLMs can reason about meaning across multiple characters in historical Chinese ritual inscriptions.
