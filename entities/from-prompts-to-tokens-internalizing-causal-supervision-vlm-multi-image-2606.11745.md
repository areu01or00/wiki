# From Prompts to Tokens: Internalizing Causal Supervision in Vision-Language Model for Multi-Image Causal Reasoning

## Overview
Visual causal reasoning is essential for understanding and intervening in the physical world. This paper addresses how VLMs can internalize causal supervision for multi-image counterfactual reasoning — identifying causal variables from visual inputs and reasoning over intervention effects.
