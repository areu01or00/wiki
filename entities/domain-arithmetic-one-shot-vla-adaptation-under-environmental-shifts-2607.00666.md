# Domain Arithmetic: One-Shot VLA Adaptation under Environmental Shifts

## Overview
Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models often fail when environmental shifts occur — changes in camera pose or different but similar robots. Adapting to shifted environments typically requires costly multiple-demonstration training per task. This paper proposes an analogy-based method (Domain Arithmetic) that adapts VLA models to new environments with a single demonstration by learning a transferable domain invariant representation.

## Key Contributions
- One-shot VLA adaptation under environmental shifts (camera pose, robot morphology)
- Analogy-based Domain Arithmetic method
- Single demonstration transfer — no multi-demonstration training required
- Domain-invariant representation learning for cross-robot generalization
