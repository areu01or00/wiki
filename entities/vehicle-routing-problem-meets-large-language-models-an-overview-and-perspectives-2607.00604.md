# Vehicle Routing Problem Meets Large Language Models: An Overview and Perspectives

## Overview
This paper provides a comprehensive overview of the intersection between Large Language Models and the Vehicle Routing Problem (VRP) — a central NP-hard optimization problem in logistics automation, transportation scheduling, and industrial decision-making. The authors survey how LLMs can be applied to route planning, constraint handling, and dynamic re-routing in transportation systems.

## Key Facts
- **Authors**: Xiu, Xianchao; Shen, Chong; Zhu, Yanjiao + 1
- **Year**: 2026
- **arXiv**: [2607.00604](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00604)

## Key Contributions
- Comprehensive survey of LLM approaches to VRP and its variants (time windows, vehicle capacities, pick-up-and-delivery)
- Analysis of LLM strengths in natural-language constraint specification and dynamic route re-planning
- Identification of open challenges in LLM-based combinatorial optimization
