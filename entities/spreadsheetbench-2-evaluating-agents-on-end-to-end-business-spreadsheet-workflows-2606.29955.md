# SpreadsheetBench 2: Evaluating Agents on End-to-End Business Spreadsheet Workflows

## Overview
Spreadsheets are widely used for business analysis, financial modeling, reporting, and decision-making. Most existing spreadsheet benchmarks evaluate isolated operations such as single-formula generation or local cell edits. SpreadsheetBench 2 is a workflow-level benchmark for spreadsheet agents that covers three task categories — generation, debugging, and visualization — evaluating end-to-end business spreadsheet workflows rather than atomic operations.

## Key Facts
- **Authors**: SpreadsheetBench Team
- **Year**: 2026
- **arXiv**: [2606.29955](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29955)

## Key Contributions
- First **end-to-end business spreadsheet workflow** benchmark for LLM agents — covers generation, debugging, and visualization chains
- Evaluates realistic business settings (financial modeling, reporting, decision-support) rather than synthetic single-operation tasks
- Distinguishes between workflow-level task completion and isolated formula/cell-level performance
- Three task categories provide multi-dimensional evaluation of agent spreadsheet capabilities
