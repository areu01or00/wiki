# From Shortcuts to Reasoning: Robust Post-Training of Theory of Mind with Reinforcement Learning

## Overview
This paper reveals that Theory of Mind (ToM) capabilities acquired through post-training are systematically confounded by pervasive "shortcut" artifacts — where RL-based ToM training exploits superficial task patterns rather than genuine mental-state reasoning. The authors demonstrate that published gains in ToM benchmarks largely disappear when these shortcut artifacts are controlled for, and propose a robust post-training methodology that isolates true ToM reasoning from shortcut-driven performance.
