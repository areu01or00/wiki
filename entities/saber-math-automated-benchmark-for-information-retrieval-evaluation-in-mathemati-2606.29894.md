# SABER-Math: Automated Benchmark for Information Retrieval Evaluation in Mathematics

## Overview
SABER-Math is a benchmark for evaluating retriever effectiveness in agentic mathematical AI systems. As agentic AI tackles complex math tasks, it relies on IR to search problem databases, theorem libraries, and educational resources. SABER-Math provides a controlled framework to isolate retriever effect on downstream mathematical reasoning performance.

## Key Facts
- **Authors**: SABER-Math Team
- **Year**: 2026
- **arXiv**: [2606.29894](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29894)

## Key Contributions
- First benchmark specifically designed to isolate retriever effectiveness in mathematical agentic AI systems
- Addresses the challenge that existing retrieval-specific benchmarks cannot directly measure downstream task performance
- Provides controlled evaluation framework for information retrieval in agentic mathematical reasoning
- Enables systematic comparison of retriever choices for math-aware agent pipelines
