# RewardUQ: A Unified Framework for Uncertainty-Aware Reward Models

## Overview
Proposes RewardUQ, a unified framework for uncertainty-aware reward models that captures both epistemic and aleatoric uncertainty in LLM alignment. Shows that standard pointwise reward estimates miss critical failure modes where reward models are confidently wrong. RewardUQ decomposes reward uncertainty into knowledge uncertainty (what the model doesn't know) and annotation uncertainty (labeler disagreement). Enables safer RLHF by abstaining from high-uncertainty regions of the reward landscape. First unified framework for uncertainty-aware reward modeling that decomposes epistemic from aleatoric uncertainty in LLM alignment.
