# DPBench: Structural Determinants of Multi-Agent LLM Coordination Under Simultaneous Resource Contention

## Overview
DPBench is a benchmark for evaluating coordination in multi-agent systems built from large language models. Existing benchmarks measure task-level success under a fixed protocol; DPBench characterizes the structural conditions under which coordination succeeds or fails at all, specifically under simultaneous resource contention scenarios.

## Key Facts
- **arXiv**: [2602.13255](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.13255)
- **Theme**: multi-agent coordination / resource contention benchmark

## Key Contributions
- First benchmark specifically measuring structural conditions for multi-agent LLM coordination success/failure
- Identifies three protocol-level variables determining coordination success under simultaneous resource contention
- Establishes precommitment as a key coordination mechanism for LLMs
