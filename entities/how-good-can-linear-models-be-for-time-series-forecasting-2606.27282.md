# How Good Can Linear Models Be for Time-Series Forecasting?

## Overview
Challenges the trend toward large transformer/ foundation model architectures for time-series; shows most of the accuracy gap can be closed with properly tuned Ridge regression plus better preprocessing, at far lower computational cost.
