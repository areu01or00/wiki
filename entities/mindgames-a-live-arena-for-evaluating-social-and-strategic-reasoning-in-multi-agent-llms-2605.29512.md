# MINDGAMES: A Live Arena for Evaluating Social and Strategic Reasoning in Multi-Agent LLMs

## Overview
MINDGAMES introduces a multi-game arena and evaluation platform for LLM agents that operationalizes complementary reasoning dimensions — theory of mind, strategic deception, coalition formation, and iterative deception — in sustained multi-agent interactions. Unlike static vignette-based ToM benchmarks, MINDGAMES captures the dynamic, multi-turn social reasoning required in real-world multi-agent deployments.

## Key Facts
- **Authors**: Wang, Kevin; Thöni, Anna; Kempinski, Benjamin; Cheng, Bobby; Yao, Jianzhu; Finch, Benjamin; Gao, Leon G.
- **Year**: 2025
- **arXiv**: [2605.29512](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29512)

## Key Contributions
- Multi-game arena operationalizing complementary social reasoning dimensions (ToM, strategic deception, coalition formation, iterative deception)
- Live evaluation platform vs. static vignettes — captures sustained multi-turn social reasoning
- Four complementary game environments for orthogonal probing of social intelligence
- First benchmark to evaluate social+strategic reasoning jointly in multi-agent LLM settings
