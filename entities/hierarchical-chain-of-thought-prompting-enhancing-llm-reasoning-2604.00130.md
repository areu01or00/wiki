# Hierarchical Chain-of-Thought Prompting: Enhancing LLM Reasoning Performance and Efficiency

## Overview
Chain-of-Thought (CoT) prompting has significantly improved the reasoning capabilities of large language models (LLMs). However, conventional CoT often relies on unstructured, flat reasoning chains. This paper introduces Hierarchical Chain-of-Thought prompting, which organizes reasoning into hierarchical structures to improve both performance and computational efficiency.

## Key Facts
- **arXiv**: [2604.00130](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.00130)
- **Date**: 2026-04

## Key Contributions
- Hierarchical organization of chain-of-thought reasoning for LLM reasoning tasks
- Improves both accuracy and efficiency compared to flat CoT approaches
- Reduces redundant reasoning steps through structured hierarchical decomposition
