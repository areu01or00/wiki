# SoK: Robustness in Large Language Models against Jailbreak Attacks

## Overview
This Systematization of Knowledge paper presents the first comprehensive taxonomy of jailbreak attacks and defenses for LLMs, introducing the Security Cube as a unified multi-dimensional evaluation framework. The paper benchmarks 13 representative attacks and 5 defenses, establishing a clear landscape view of jailbreak attacks, defenses, automated judges, and LLM vulnerabilities. Key findings include that single-layer defenses often trade security for usability, and that the multidimensional nature of LLM security cannot be captured by narrow metrics like attack success rate alone.
