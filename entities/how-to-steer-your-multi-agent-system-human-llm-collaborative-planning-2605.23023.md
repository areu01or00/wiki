# How to Steer Your Multi-Agent System: Human-LLM Collaborative Planning

## Overview
AMBIPOM formalizes a design space for human-LLM co-planning interactions along three axes — mode (semantic vs. structural), scope (global vs. targeted), and level (low vs. high-level edits) — and implements process-level supervision enabling users to verify and revise intermediate reasoning, not just final outputs. Includes a user study characterizing hybrid workflows and effort-control-risk trade-offs.

## Key Facts
- **arXiv**: [2605.23023](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23023)

## Key Contributions
- Formalized 3-axis design space for human-LLM co-planning: mode × scope × level
- AMBIPOM prototype supporting process-level supervision through both semantic and structural interactions
- User study reveals hybrid workflows and effort-control-risk trade-offs in multi-agent steering
- Process-level revision strategies outperform outcome-only supervision for complex orchestrated tasks
