2. **[Prune as You Generate: Online Rollout Pruning for Faster and Better RLVR](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24840)** — Haobo Xu — First online rollout pruning technique for RLVR (GRPO/DAPO) pipelines — dynamically eliminates low-advantage tokens during RLVR sampling, reducing rollout count while maintaining final reward. Addresses sparse advantage problem where most GRPO samples are near all-correct or all-incorrect. **First online rollout pruning for RLVR in the wiki.**

3. **[Accordion-Thinking: Self-Regulated Step Summaries for Efficient and Readable LLM Reasoning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.03249)** — Zhicheng Yang — First end-to-end framework for self-regulated CoT step granularity via dynamic KV summarization — LLM learns to compress less-critical reasoning steps while preserving accuracy on critical inference junctures. Reduces attention complexity from O(n²) per step to variable granularity. **First self-regulated CoT step compression paper in the wiki.**
